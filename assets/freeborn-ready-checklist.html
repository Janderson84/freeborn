72-Hour Family Preparedness Checklist

The same framework used in municipal emergency planning. Work through this over one weekend.

Water (1 gallon per person per day)

  • 3-day supply of water per family member (minimum)
  • Water storage containers (food-grade, sealed)
  • Water purification tablets or filter (LifeStraw or equivalent)
  • Collapsible water containers for refilling

Food (72-Hour Supply)

  • Non-perishable food for 3 days per person
  • Manual can opener
  • Camping stove with fuel canister (or propane stove)
  • Lightweight cookware
  • Disposable plates, utensils
  • 72-hour meal plan printed and in your kit

Light and Power

  • Flashlight with extra batteries (or hand-crank)
  • Headlamp (hands-free in emergencies)
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank weather radio
  • Fully charged power bank (10,000 mAh minimum)
  • Solar charger (optional but recommended)
  • Candles and waterproof matches

First Aid and Health

  • Complete first aid kit (bandages, gauze, antiseptic, tape)
  • Prescription medications: 7-day supply per person
  • OTC medications: pain reliever, antidiarrheal, antacid
  • N95 masks (at least 2 per person)
  • Latex gloves
  • Emergency blankets (mylar, 1 per person)
  • First aid manual

Documents and Financial

  • Copies of IDs, passports, birth certificates in waterproof pouch
  • Insurance cards and policy numbers
  • Bank account and contact information (written, not just digital)
  • Cash in small bills ($100+ recommended)
  • Emergency contacts list (printed)

Communication and Navigation

  • Battery-powered NOAA weather radio
  • Paper maps of your area and surrounding counties
  • Family emergency communication plan (laminated)
  • Out-of-area contact person designated
  • Meeting point agreed upon (home + backup location)
  • Whistle for signaling

Shelter and Warmth

  • Warm clothing for each person (layering system)
  • Emergency mylar blankets (1 per person)
  • Rain ponchos
  • Sturdy shoes in kit (not flip flops)
  • Work gloves

Tools and Supplies

  • Multi-tool or Swiss Army knife
  • Duct tape
  • Paracord (50 feet)
  • Tarp or emergency shelter
  • Dust masks or N95 respirators
  • Hand sanitizer and wet wipes
  • 72-hour backpack or duffel bag, packed and accessible
