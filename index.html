Your Free 72-Hour Preparedness Checklist

The same framework used in municipal emergency planning. Print it, put it on your fridge, work through it this weekend.

  • 1 gallon of water per person per day (minimum 3 days)
  • 72-hour supply of non-perishable food per family member
  • Flashlight with extra batteries (or hand-crank)
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank weather radio
  • First aid kit and prescription medications (7-day supply)
  • Important documents in a waterproof pouch
  • Cash in small bills (ATMs fail in outages)
  • Warm clothing and emergency blankets for each person
  • Fully charged phone power bank
  • Family emergency communication plan and meeting point
  • Multi-tool or Swiss Army knife
  • 72-hour kit bag, packed and accessible
