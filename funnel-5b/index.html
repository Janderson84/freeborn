Why I Created This

How My Grandmother's Garden Became My Family's Medicine Cabinet

My grandmother grew up in rural Appalachia before rural healthcare existed. She raised 7 children using a combination of practical medicine, common sense, and a garden full of plants she'd inherited knowledge of from her own mother. When she moved in with us in her eighties, I started asking questions.

What I learned changed how I think about health. The Backyard Apothecary Guide is my attempt to document the foundational plant medicines every family should know — the ones that are easy to grow, scientifically supported, and genuinely useful for everyday health.
