While You Wait

One Question Worth Thinking About

Your writer is going to ask you for the specific numbers behind your work. Not job descriptions — evidence.

Before they email you: spend 15 minutes writing down the three most specific, measurable things you've accomplished in the last 5 years. Revenue influenced, costs reduced, teams led, projects shipped, customers served. Dollar figures, percentages, headcounts, timelines.

The more specific you can be, the stronger your resume will be. "Reduced costs by 22% ($1.4M)" will always outperform "improved operational efficiency."
